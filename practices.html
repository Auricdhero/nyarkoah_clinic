our practice

The clinic provides world class pediatric, men's health and wellness services. Apart from offering state of the art telemedicine technology with partner hospitals in the state of Georgia, USA, the clinic will provide comprehensive diagnostic tools to identify health problems in men over 40.

At Nyakoa Clinic we believe in quality healthcare delivery. We house a first class highly skilled team of professionals who will deliver the best in healthcare and customer service and will handle all aspects of your visit confidentially and exceptionally.

The clinic will do everything possible to meet patient’s appointment and scheduling needs, answer questions, provide patient education, offer daily SMS health tips, and direct patients to other health care resources.

our doctor

Dr. John Opata Mate-Kole

Dr. John Opata Mate-Kole is a Board Certified Pediatrician and a Fellow of the American Academy of Pediatrics. He obtained his medical degree from the University of Ghana Medical School.

He subsequently pursued his Pediatric Specialty training at the College of Physicians and Surgeons,Columbia University at Harlem Hospital.

After completing his Residency, he worked as a Clinical Research Fellow in Neonatology at the University of Connecticut Health Center in Farmington,CT.

He later became the Medical Director at the Goldstein Children’s Health Center at the Stamford Hospital in Connecticut.

Dr. Mate-Kole is a certified Neonatal Resuscitation Program and Pediatrics Advanced Life support instructor. His areas of interest are in the management of premature infants and Asthma control in Children and Adolescents.

He is a strong advocate of Men’s Health and Wellness. He retired from the United States Federal Government in April 2014 and is currently a contract Locums Pediatrician in Georgia.

Dr Mate-Kole is the founder of the Nyakoa Medical Group LLC, of Georgia and Ghana. The Nyakoa Clinic in Ghana is named after his late mother Grace Nyakoa Mate-Kole, who loved to help people and was a pillar of the Ghanaian society.

He is also the Medical Director of Emofra Africa, a Non Governmental Organization in Ghana and the US.
